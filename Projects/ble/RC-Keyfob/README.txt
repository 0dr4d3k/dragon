This code goes on a CC2540 MiniDK keyfob and controls the car steering and throttle.
Push and hold both buttons to scan and connect to a peripheral advertising with certain data. Green blink means connected
Push and hold both again to diconnect. Red blink means disconnected.
When connected:
Push and hold right button to enable accelerometer. Green blink means accelerometer enabled.
Push and hold right button again to disable accelerometer. Orange blink means accelerometer disabled.
